About ADM

Welcome to AFIT Digital Market

AFIT Digital Market (ADM) is a campus-focused online marketplace explicitly tailored to connect students, small business owners, and service providers within the AFIT community. We make it simple to discover, promote, and access goods right where you are.

Our Mission

  • Empower student entrepreneurs to reach a wider audience.
  • Make buying and selling on campus secure, fast, and organized.
  • Centralize the scattered WhatsApp market into one trusted hub.

How It Works

We serve purely as a discovery and listing platform. Vendors showcase their best items, and buyers can easily browse and contact them directly. ADM does not process payments or handle deliveries.
